About

Deep Sensors Lab (DSL) was established in 2018 in the Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay. Since then, it is being convened by Prof.Pramod Murali. The lab is located in the Annex building of the department. At DSL, we foster innovation in diverse fields such as LiDAR, Mixed Signal VLSI Design, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and MEMS. DSL is enabled with some of the state of the art equipment like Optical Table, High-end Microscope, Spectrum Analyzer, Oscilloscope, High Speed Server and High Performance GPU.
